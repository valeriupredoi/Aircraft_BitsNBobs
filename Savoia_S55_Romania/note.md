## Savoia SM.55 (S.55) in Romanian service

101 Squadron, Lake Siutghiol, Constanta, Romania. In 1934, six (seven) S.55s were acquired by Romania, used on the Black Sea as long-range scouts, light bombers, and torpedo bombers. These were provided the ASSO 500 engines and very similar to the “X” standard. They performed these largely uneventful missions until 1945, but they were retired from frontline in 1942, due to their age and slow speed.
