## F-16BM Romanian Air Force 1649

- Previous service with Royal Norwegian Air Force (RNoAF): original BuNo 80-3691; original build: Fokker for the RNoAF, serial 691, type F-16B Block 15D, build date 31 March 1983, entered service 6 April 1983, MLU upgrade to Block 20 between 1999 and 2002, last serial 691, with 331 skv, painted in special Normandy scheme in May 2019 (75th anniversary of D-Day);
- Current service with Romanian Air Force (RoAF): entered service 11 July 2024, part of FMS Program: Peace Carpathian III (32 F-16AM/BM from Norway), with Escadrila 48 Vanatoare (48th Fighter Squadron, of WW2 Royal Romanian Air Force fame, part of Grupul 9 Vanatoare, led by Capt. Av. Alexandru Serbanescu, with Lt. Av. Ion Dobran a notable member of the 48th, and re-established on 19 April 2024, with the first arrivals of RNoAF F-16s), at 71st Air Base "General Emanoil Ionescu", Campia Turzii, Cluj, Romania.
