

## Undesrtanding the markings of Operation Joint Endeavour

- "1D-2-F3 A16 <11":  1st Armoured Division, 2nd Battalion, 3rd Field Arty Regiment, A battery, vehicle 16 (2 January 1996)
  - location: Zupanja, Croatia, towards the pontoon bridge over Sava River towards Bosnia, arrived by rail from Kirch-Goens, Germany
- "1D-4-F29 A12 ^21": 1st Armoured Division, 4th Battalion, 29th Field Arty Regiment, A battery, vehicle 12 (22 April 1996)
- "1D-4-F29 A14 ^21": 1st Armoured Division, 4th Battalion, 29th Field Arty Regiment, A battery, vehicle 14 (22 April 1996)
  - location: Camp Steele/Donje Ceparde, Bosnia
- "1D-2-F3 A14 ^11":  1st Armoured Division, 2nd Battalion, 3rd Field Arty Regiment, A battery, vehicle 14 (5 January 1996)
  - location: Zupanja, Croatia
- "1D-4-F29 Bxx ^22": 1st Armoured Division, 4th Battalion, 29th Field Arty Regiment, 1st Platoon, B battery, vehicle xx (24 May 1996)
  - location: Glamoc, Bosnia
