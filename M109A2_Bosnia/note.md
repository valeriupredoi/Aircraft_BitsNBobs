

## Undesrtanding the markings of Operation Joint Endeavour

M109s deployed to Bosnia part of IFOR/Operation Joint Endeavour had large numbers and chevrons on either side of the hull, and at the rear. It appears those were numbers that denoted the unit and battery. E.g. ^22 = 4th Battalion, Bravo (B) Battery.

  - location: Zupanja, Croatia, towards the pontoon bridge over Sava River towards Bosnia, arrived by rail from Kirch-Goens, Germany
- P2: "1D-4-F29 A12 ^21": 1st Armoured Division, 4th Battalion, 29th Field Arty Regiment, A battery, vehicle 12 (Monday, 22 April 1996)
- P3: "1D-4-F29 A14 ^21": 1st Armoured Division, 4th Battalion, 29th Field Arty Regiment, A battery, vehicle 14 (Monday, 22 April 1996)
