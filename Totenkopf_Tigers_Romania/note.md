The 9. (schwere) Panzer-Kompanie/SS-Panzer-Regiment 3, belonging to the 3rd SS Panzer Division Totenkopf was a specialized Tiger I heavy tank company formed in 1943. Unlike many other SS-Panzer-Regiments that incorporated their Tiger companies into formal schwere SS-Panzer-Abteilungen (Heavy SS Panzer Battalions), the 9th Company remained attached to the Totenkopf Panzer Regiment throughout much of the war.

Tiger regiment in Romania:

- 3 April 1944  Totenkopf retreats behind the Dnestr into Romania
- 14 April 1944 Enroute to Tiraspol when the rail line is broken, all remaining Tigers blown up to prevent capture
- 2 May 1944 	3 Tigers delivered
- 20 May 1944 	III./Panzerregiment Grossdeutschland transfers 4 Tigers to 9./
- 7 June 1944 	2 more Tigers delivered
- 9 June 1944 	6 Tigers are received from s.SS-Pz.Abt. 103, total of 15 on strength
- 7 July 1944 	Entrained for transfer to Poland
